About HBTesting

HBTesting is a UK-based testing consultancy founded by Harry Bevan, a software testing specialist with hands-on experience across manual testing, automation, CI/CD pipelines, and AI system validation.

The consultancy works with development and delivery teams to embed effective testing practices that scale. While HBTesting operates as a small consultancy today, it is designed to grow alongside client needs without losing technical depth or quality.

Specialist AI System Testing

A core focus of HBTesting is the testing of AI-enabled systems using automated and repeatable frameworks. This includes validating not only functionality, but also reliability, consistency, and risk across end-to-end AI workflows.

  • End-to-end testing of AI and chatbot systems
  • Automated frameworks for validating AI outputs and behaviours
  • Test case generation and refinement using AI assistance
  • Risk-based testing of machine learning and AI-driven features
  • Support for integrating AI testing into existing CI/CD pipelines

The approach is pragmatic and transparent, using AI where it adds value while ensuring results remain understandable, auditable, and maintainable.
